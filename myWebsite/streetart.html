About Bristol's Street Art

Here is some of the famous art seen on the local streets of Bristol

Banksy

Banksy is now a world-famous artist, whose work has sold for hundreds of thousands of pounds. But once, he was just another kid on the streets of Bristol with a can of spray paint in his hands. As a result, some of his earliest work is hidden around the city, and spotting it is an absolute must for fans.


Nelson Street

In the centre of the city, the area around Nelson Street has some impressive large scale murals from some of the best known artists around. The likes of Conor Harrington, Aryz, Stik, Pixel Pancho, Nick Walker and El Mac can all be seen in a relatively small area.


Upfest

The Upfest Gallery, on North Street, Bedminster, is the longest running urban art gallery in Bristol and, with its exhibitions of top international street artists, is a fantastic place to discover the Banksys of tomorrow.
